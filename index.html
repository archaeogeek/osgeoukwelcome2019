OSGeo == The Open Source Geospatial Foundation

OSGeo:UK == The Open Source Geospatial Foundation UK Local Chapter

OSGeo is a global foundation with a mission to support open source software and people that use it

There are ~30,000 members world-wide, ranging from developers to users, global corporations to individuals

The UK membership is a bit smaller than that

Events like FOSS4GUK are a vital part of OSGeo's remit

"A meeting of the tribes"

OSGeo's role also includes promoting sustainable properly-licensed software, like QGIS and PostGIS

also smaller packages such as pgrouting and Loader

Come to the OSGeo:UK AGM, at 1pm today to find out more!
